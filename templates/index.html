Welcome to SupportIQ!

SupportIQ is a platform focused on helping children suffering with learning disabilities, mainly focused on learning disabilities related to low IQ.

Find Out More

Let us tell you where Magic Happens!


Assistive technology (AT) is available to help individuals with many types of disabilities — from cognitive problems to doing basic tasks. The use of technology to enhance learning is an effective approach for many children. Additionally, students with LD often experience greater success when they are allowed to use their abilities (strengths) to work around their disabilities (challenges). AT tools combine the best of both of these practices.

What do we offer you?


Take A Test

A simple test to identify the severity of symptoms and help you make the decision of opting for professional help.
Parent Forum Portal

A platform for various individuals to share resources and inputs with one another on a direct one-to-one basis
Schedule Follower

Use our scheduler to create schedules and monitor your child's progress and routines. Daily routines and tasks can be monitored and can be inputted along side your child.

Our Platform is here to help the parents of those children with special needs

We know how stressful and confusing this journey can be. We know that every parent here wants to give their children the best and hence we want to help you give that best. SupportIQ solely exists to bring all such parents together and share their inputs, resources and support with one another. No one wants to feel alone or left out in their battles and we want to help you fight yours.

For those parents uninitiated or unsure of whether their children suffer from learning disabilities related to low IQ, try taking our tailormade test to help ease your doubts.
